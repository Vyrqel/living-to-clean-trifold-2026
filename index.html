The Problem

Over 1,340 active Superfund sites hold complex mixtures of heavy metals, solvents, petroleum, pesticides, pharmaceuticals, and more. Chemical remediation moves contaminants rather than destroying them. No single organism handles the full contamination spectrum — this consortium does.

★ Centerpiece: Inter-Species Mercury Trafficking Cascade

A proposed 4-stage pipeline to eliminate Hg⁰ volatilization and concentrate Hg²⁺ in harvestable fungal biomass. Computational proposal — not experimentally validated.

⚠

Scientific Integrity Disclosure: The mercury cascade and all novel protein designs (Atx1-Hg, CusA-Hg, CusF-Hg) are theoretical proposals grounded in structural biochemistry and HSAB principles. No in vitro or in vivo testing has been performed. All other consortium members are characterized organisms with published literature support.

1
Metarhizium robertsii (ΔMIR) lit. basis

Demethylation

MMD enzyme (PNAS 2022) converts methylmercury → Hg²⁺. MIR deletion prevents Hg⁰ escape.

2
Cupriavidus metallidurans (ΔmerA) speculative

Hg²⁺ Export

CusA-Hg / CusF-Hg efflux system (novel engineered proteins) exports Hg²⁺ extracellularly into the shared pool.

3
Aspergillus niger (Atx1-Hg) speculative

Extracellular Capture

Atx1-Hg (novel chaperone) captures extracellular Hg²⁺ and shuttles it into fungal cells for sequestration.

4
Aspergillus niger (MT2A / PgpdA) lit. basis

Sequestration & Harvest

MT2A metallothionein constitutively sequesters Hg²⁺. Fungal biomass harvested to remove mercury from the system permanently.

Novel Engineered Proteins

Three computationally-derived proteins designed from structural biochemistry and HSAB (Hard-Soft Acid-Base) principles. Not yet synthesized or experimentally validated.

UNVALIDATED

Atx1-Hg — Hg²⁺ Chaperone (A. niger)

Modified copper chaperone retargeted for extracellular Hg²⁺ capture
  • MxCxxC → MCxCC: tricoordinate Hg²⁺ binding site (MerR template, C82/C117/C126)
  • Gly spacers allow backbone flexibility for Hg²⁺-preferred linear/trigonal geometry
  • Interaction surface redesigned to dock MT2A rather than native Cu-ATPases
UNVALIDATED

CusA-Hg & CusF-Hg — Hg²⁺ Efflux (C. metallidurans)

RND efflux system retargeted from Cu⁺/Ag⁺ selectivity to Hg²⁺
  • CusA-Hg: Met relay → Cys (M573C, M623C, M672C) — contiguous thiolate-lined channel
  • CusF-Hg: HxMxM → HxCxC — Hg²⁺-selective from the periplasmic entry point
  • Basis: HSAB theory — Hg²⁺ (soft acid) has extreme thiolate (soft base) affinity
Why Multi-Kingdom?

Multi-Kingdom Architecture

Fungi (hyphal scaffold + extracellular enzymes) paired with bacteria (metabolic versatility, anaerobic capability). Neither kingdom alone covers the full pollutant spectrum.

Fungal Highways

Fusarium and Trichoderma hyphae act as physical conduits — bacteria travel along fungal filaments to reach otherwise inaccessible contaminated soil zones.

Zero Volatilization Design

All Hg⁰-producing pathways deleted (ΔMIR, ΔmerA). Mercury stays concentrated and harvestable — never escapes as atmospheric vapor.

Compatibility Engineering

Trichoderma peptaibol + chitinase deletions stop co-species killing. Penicillium pcb cluster deletion protects bacterial members from antibiotic exposure.
