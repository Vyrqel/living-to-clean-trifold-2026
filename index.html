★ Centerpiece: Inter-Species Mercury Trafficking Cascade

A proposed 4-stage pipeline designed to eliminate atmospheric Hg⁰ volatilization and concentrate Hg²⁺ in harvestable fungal biomass.

⚠

Computational proposal — not experimentally validated. The inter-species cascade and all novel protein designs (Atx1-Hg, CusA-Hg, CusF-Hg) are theoretical, grounded in structural biochemistry and HSAB principles.

1
Metarhizium robertsii (ΔMIR) lit. basis

Demethylation

MMD enzyme (PNAS 2022) converts methylmercury → Hg²⁺. MIR deletion blocks Hg⁰ escape.

2
Cupriavidus metallidurans (ΔmerA) speculative

Hg²⁺ Export

CusA-Hg/CusF-Hg efflux system exports Hg²⁺ extracellularly. Protein designs computationally derived.

CusA-Hg / CusF-Hg — unvalidated
3
Aspergillus niger (Atx1-Hg) speculative

Extracellular Capture

Atx1-Hg captures extracellular Hg²⁺ and shuttles it inward. Binding motif redesign is theoretical.

Atx1-Hg — unvalidated
4
Aspergillus niger (MT2A / PgpdA) lit. basis

Sequestration & Harvest

MT2A metallothionein constitutively expressed. Biomass harvested to remove mercury from system.

Novel Engineered Proteins — Computational Proposals
⚠

No experimental validation. Protein designs are theoretical — derived from structural biochemistry, HSAB principles, and homology to characterized systems. Not yet synthesized, expressed, or assayed.

Atx1-Hg — Hg²⁺ Chaperone (A. niger)

UNVALIDATED
  • MxCxxC → MCxCC motif: third Cys added, spacing reduced — tricoordinate Hg²⁺ binding site
  • Gly spacers allow backbone flexibility for Hg²⁺-preferred linear/trigonal geometry
  • Interaction surface redesigned to dock MT2A instead of native Cu-ATPases
  • Template: MerR C82/C117/C126 coordination geometry (experimentally characterized)

CusA-Hg & CusF-Hg — Hg²⁺ Efflux (C. metallidurans)

UNVALIDATED
  • CusA-Hg: transmembrane Met relay → Cys (M573C, M623C, M672C) — continuous thiolate channel
  • CusF-Hg: periplasmic chaperone HxMxM → HxCxC — Hg²⁺-selective from entry point
  • Basis: HSAB theory — Hg²⁺ (soft acid) has extreme thiolate (soft base) affinity
Competitive Advantages

Multi-Kingdom Architecture

Fungi (hyphal scaffold + extracellular enzymes) paired with bacteria (metabolic versatility, anaerobic capability) for complementary attack across all contamination zones simultaneously.

Fungal Highways

Fusarium and Trichoderma hyphae act as physical conduits letting specialist bacteria reach otherwise inaccessible deep zones in contaminated soil.

Zero Volatilization Design

All Hg⁰-producing pathways deleted (MIR in M. robertsii; MerA in C. metallidurans). Mercury stays in the system — concentrated, captured, and harvested.

Consortium Compatibility Engineering

Trichoderma peptaibol synthetase + chitinase genes deleted. Penicillium pcb cluster deleted to protect bacterial members. Engineered to cooperate, not compete.
